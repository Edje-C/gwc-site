A group of women can begin a war with their inner voices and fight in the world as if they were a fierce tiger. The following are women who have impacted history in the United States:
  1. Harriet Tubman shows more than a face of courage. She risked her safety when handling over 300 slaves through the Underground Railroad. She grew up as a house slave and after she was freed, she helped escort slaves from the South to the North.
  2. Rosa Park's refusal to surrender her seat to a white passenger on a public bus Montgomery, Alabama, made controversy because of racial differences. It spurred on a citywide boycott and helped launch nationwide efforts to end segregation of public facilities because of her relentless effort.
  3. Maya Angelou's writings expressed feelings that others may not know how to explain. Her love of poems and writing has inspired many people to also share their emotions through writing, as well.
  4. Michelle Obama is a lawyer, advocate, and the former First Lady. She continues to use her voice to represent all women of different color and race. She also helped start programs for children to start eating healthier and exercise daily. As a result, there was an decline in obesity in children.
  5. Nora Ephron was a journalist, essayist, playwriter, novelist, screenwriter, producer, and director. She battled the industry's gender inequality. She was determined to pursue her career even though she was often looked down upon because she was just a "woman". She is famously known for directing the movie When Harry Met Sally. Actors like Cloë Grace Moretz and Judy Greer said that they have been impacted by her role in a male dominated working field. She truly serves as an inspiration to not only the women aspiring to become actors, but women in general in the generations to come.
A Woman Who Inspires Me Everyday
  • My mother inspires me everyday to become the best that I could be. She is my motivator, helper, mentor, and best friend. Even though she immigrated to the United States from Ecuador at age 19, she showed me that there are endless possibilites to learn and grow. She plays the role of both mother and father to my sister and I and she never fails to care for our needs. Most importantly, she loves us no matter what. You see, even though I sometimes disappoint her, I know she'll always be there for me. She is a selfless, fearless, and a hard working woman who puts the needs of others before her own. Words cannot describe the amount of honor and love I have for my mother. She is truly my inspiration and heroe.
